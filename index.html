lung dart

Lung Dart are Tim Clay and James Rapson, their music is centred around ideas of memory and spans the realms of live instrumentation, sample-based productions, experimental music and pop. They have released a mini album, two EPs, and a mixtape alongside commissioned work which includes scoring The White Girl (2017), a feature film directed by Christopher Doyle, as well as various short films and commercial projects.

Their NTS Radio show airs every fourth Monday at 10pm.

Listen, buy and look at Spotify, Bandcamp and Instagram.
