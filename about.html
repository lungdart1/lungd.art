London/East Midlands


Lung Dart's music spans live instrumentation, sample-based productions, experimental music and pop.

Alongside their own releases they have scored artworks and films that have been shown at Tate Britain, Tate Modern, Barbican Centre, BFI London Film Festival, Somerset House, Berlin Film Festival and many others.

They have performed live at venues including Tate Britain, Tate Modern, LSO St Lukes, De La Warr Pavilion, Liverpool Philharmonic Hall and Sage Gateshead.

Their NTS Radio show airs every fourth Monday at 10pm.
