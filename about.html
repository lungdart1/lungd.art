London/East Midlands


Our music spans live instrumentation, sample-based productions, experimental music and pop.

Alongside our own releases we have scored artworks and films that have been shown at Cannes Film Festival, Tate Britain, Tate Modern, Barbican Centre, BFI London Film Festival, Berlin Film Festival and many others.

We have performed live at venues including Tate Britain, Tate Modern, LSO St Lukes, De La Warr Pavilion, Liverpool Philharmonic Hall and Sage Gateshead.

Our NTS Radio show has been broadcast at 10pm every fourth Monday since 2018.
